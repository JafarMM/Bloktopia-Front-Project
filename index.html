INTRODUCING
BLOKTOPIA

THE HOME OF
CRYPTO

Bloktopia is a Skyscraper made up of 21 levels to pay
recognition to 21 million Bitcoin. Token holders will be
known as Bloktopians. It will act as a central hub and
welcome all levels of crypto experience. For the first time
ever, users will have access to crypto information and
immersive content all in one place. Bloktopians will be able
to earn revenue through real estate ownership, advertising
revenue, play games, build networks and much more. By
using the world’s most advanced real time 3D Creation
Engine, Bloktopia will leverage this technology to create
stunning visualisations and user experience.
